Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. make a nest with the flour on a clean work surface
  2. use your hands to gently mix in the flour
  3. Then, knead!
  4. When the dough comes together, shape it into a ball and wrap it in plastic wrap
  5. After the dough rests, slice it into 4 pieces.
  6. Then, run it through the widest setting of your pasta maker
  7. Then, fold it in half lengthwise to form a rectangle.
  8. Once you've folded the dough, roll it out to your desired thickness
  9. Finally, cut and cook the pasta
